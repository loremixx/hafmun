COMMITTEE OF AI (UNHLAB)

As artificial intelligence grows at an unprecedented rate, the world has urgent questions about how to manage its risks and its potential. The UN High-Level Advisory Body on AI promotes cooperation among global stakeholders, policymakers, companies and specialists for the consideration of models for the safe, ethical, and inclusive development of more advanced AI systems.

Delegates will debate about ethical boundaries, principles of transparency, AI ethics, AI governance, and ways of implementing global regulation for artificial general intelligence and artificial superintelligence. From existential security to ethical alignment, from openness to environmental responsibility, this committee will address serious, cross-disciplinary debate. Delegates must propose policies that balance innovation and accountability, before AGI and ASI systems become uncontrollable realities.

Agenda Item: Regulations on Potential Artificial General Intelligence and Superintelligent AI Models
