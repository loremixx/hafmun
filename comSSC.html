The Shadow Security Council (SSC)

The Shadow Security Council is a classified, unofficial branch of the United Nations created to address the rise of covert conflicts, drone warfare, and untraceable assassinations. Operating entirely behind closed doors, with no public transcripts or press briefings, the SSC investigates and sets norms for clandestine operations conducted by states, intelligence agencies, and private military contractors.

This committee challenges delegates to navigate a world where diplomacy is hidden in secrecy, and accountability is elusive. Delegates will grapple with ethical dilemmas, strategic deception, and the limits of international law in the shadow wars shaping global security.
